About Me

/life-of-an-extroverted-introvert/

My name is Quang. I was born and raised in Vietnam. I had moved to the US in early 2018 and lived in Virginia with my grandma for four months before I came to California. Since I came here, I've been experiencing a tremendous amount of challenges that require me to get out of my comfort zone more, which made me grown gradually through times. Not only that, but also faced with new friends from another culture has completely transformed me into a different person. I consider myself an introvert. I'm not a conversation-initiator; I've always been in a situation in which others come and talk to me. By the time I started to realize that it's not good for me to behave like that. I began to step out of my closed-circle and lived like an extrovert. That was the most significant decision that I have to make, and I'm fully satisfied with how it emerges into me.

And that leads me to now, and I am a Freelance iOS Developer in Orange County, California. I develop high-quality apps for Apple mobile devices. Having experience in working with clients to build and deliver products that satisfy their needs and demands. Since beginning my journey into iOS Development, my dedication to the build and motivation to grow should lead me to exceptional projects and experiences.
